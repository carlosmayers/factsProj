Dry Skin

Always begin by first washing your face. Always prime your skin before applying any makeup.
Following that, you should moisturize your skin.
Use makeup that is meant for dry skin. Liquid foundation should be used over powder foundation.
Always spray your face with a setting spray.

Oily Skin

Always apply a primer after washing your face. Only apply powder to areas that are shiny.
Going overboard could backfire by making your pores push out more oil.

A tip to help control your oil is to carry blotting pads with you. Use makeup and cleansers without oil.
Use a "long wear" foundation.
Set your face with some translucent powder across your forehead, down your nose and on your chin.
Also, use a treatment face mask once or twice a week to reduce excess oils.
