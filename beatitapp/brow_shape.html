Rounded Brows
Rounded eyebrows work best for heart shaped faces to balance a prominent
Avoid rounded brows if you have a rounded face as
it makes the face appear more round.

Hard-Angled Brows
Hard-angled eyebrows are a great solution for round faces because it makes the face appear longer.
Up and down lines draw the individuals eyes up and down
and lengthens the face.
Hard Angled brows help narrow the face, especially if your face is diamond shaped.

Soft-Angled Brows
Soft-angled eyebrows work best to maintain the ideal oval face.
This is also a great eyebrow shape for square faces as it balances a square jaw.
Diamond shaped faces will
also look best with soft-angled eyebrows to narrow the face.

S-Shaped Brows
S-shaped or curved eyebrows would look best on diamond shaped faces because the curvesn
Will soften the angled face and "reduce" the widest part of the face temples.
This eyebrow shape also balances the square jaw of a square face.
Also, depending on how prominent the forehead of a heart-shaped face is
start first with a low arch, round curved brows then add more
volume to it as it "adds" more length to the forehead as well as balancing the pointy chin.

Flat Brows
Flat eyebrows is the best option for long faces because its line
would "stop" the viewer from seeing the elonged face, instead makes it appear to be shorter.
